Before You Start

Examination Rules:

  • Do not leave the camera's view during the examination!
  • Microphone must remain active for the entire duration of the test.
  • No talking, looking away frequently, or receiving assistance.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • There are 3 stages on this examination; Tongue Twisters, Phonetic Transcription, and Text.
  • One minute for each stage.
  • You can go to the next stage by clicking the "Next Stage" button or when the timer runs out.
  • Pray before you start.
